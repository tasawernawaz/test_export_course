The Goal: A Process of Ongoing Improvement (optional)

https://www.amazon.com/Goal-Process-Ongoing-Improvement/dp/0884270610 

Although this book will not be required to complete this course, we find this book extremely intuitive in helping students connect theory to practice. There will not be any Graded Problem Sets for week 1, however, Practice Problems will be based on the readings of this book.
