Definitions and Characteristics

  • Definition of bottleneck: Any resource whose capacity is less than the demand placed on it. If number of hours available less than the number of hours it is needed, it is a bottleneck.
  • Speed up the line by speeding up the bottleneck.
  • The true value of inventory is not its raw material value, to its raw material value plus "value added". It is its sales value, if it can be sold. It is zero if it cannot be sold.
  • Put QC before bottleneck so that bottleneck does not waste time working on parts that will be rejected.
  • Don't waste time at the bottleneck. Time is wasted by
      1. Idleness
      2. Working on parts that will be scrapped
      3. Working on parts that are not needed
