The Practice Problems will be based on the optional reading of the book The Goal. While the reading of the book will not be required for the course, nor will any of its contents be on Graded Problem Sets or Exams, we feel this book will really help students connect theory to practice.

The Goal, Stanley B, Eliyahu M Goldratt and Jeff Cox, 1992.
