HOMEWORK INSTRUCTIONS
You need to answer all of the questions in each of the Graded Problem Sets questions each week.  The point assignments are listed. You will have three attempts for most questions and solutions will only be provided after the deadline, unless otherwise noted.  The primary purpose of the Graded Problem Sets is to assess your knowledge of the material from this week.  
Remember that the only button that actually submits the answer is the “Submit” button. The “Save" button just saves your answer, it does not submit it. As a general rule, please regularly check your scores for each week and each question by clicking on the progress tab to avoid any bad surprises after the due date. Also remember to complete all graded assignments each week.
NOTE:  If you have any questions or issues on this Graded Problem Sets, DO NOT POST THEM ON THE DISCUSSION FORUM!   Instead send an email to 28541x-help@mit.edu stating  the Week and the Assignment in the subject line and your question or comment in the body of the email.  For example, for Week 1 Graded Problem 1 PART 1.3, your subject line would be "Question on Week 1 Graded Problem 1 PART 1.3". We will respond as soon as possible!  
As an edX student, you have certified that all work for grade (graded problems, final exam) is your own work and only your own work. This means that for this and all graded assignments:
  •  You will not submit the work of any other person or have anyone else submit work under your name.

If we become aware of any suspicious activity we reserve the right to remove credit, not award a certificate, ban from any and all POMx courses as well as notify edX for other actions. We will diligently monitor this and be very proactive. So please, do your own work.
