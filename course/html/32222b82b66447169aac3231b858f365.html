INSTRUCTIONS FOR MIDTERM EXAM

The Midterm exam consists of  problems. The problems cover the material from weeks 1 thru 7. It will be a timed exam, available during one week, but once you start the exam there is a limited time (24 hours) to complete it. You cannot pause or reset a timed exam. When there is no time remaining, the course page automatically submits your exam. Notice that you cannot complete timed exams using the edX mobile app. You have to use a web browser on a computer. 

Different parts of these problems have different levels of difficulty. If you find yourself stuck in a difficult part of a problem, we recommend that you consider moving to the easy part of the next problem. The weighting scheme is as follows:

  • Problem 1:  20 points
  • Problem 2:  20 points
  • Problem 3:  20 points
  • Problem 4:  20 points
  • Problem 5:  20 points

This is an open book , notes, and video exam. Once you start the final exam, you will have 24 hours to complete it.  Make sure your internet connectivity is working well, and that you reserve for a reasonable slot of time that allows you to complete the exam in 4 hours. If you do not complete the entire exam during the allowed time, you will lose those points associated with the questions that have not been answered. Once you access the exam, you must accept to start the exam before the timer starts. Once you accept, you can track how much time you have left in the upper right-hand corner of your screen. NOTE THAT IN ORDER TO HAVE THE ENTIRE 4 HOURS YOU MUST START THE EXAM AT LEAST 4 HOURS BEFORE THE DEADLINE November 21st at 11:00 AM. At the deadline, the exam closes.

Best of Luck!
