Optimization

This script will function just like the desktop version of Matlab. 

In order to complete the Practice Problem 2 and Graded Problem 2, we suggest users to think about clever ways of guessing \(N\) to arrive at the optimal solution.

For those who are more adept at Matlab, you can just run a for loop with a reasonable range for N, to find the optimal value.

For those of you who are familiar with the Optimization Toolbox, feel free to use fminbnd, or anything else you are familiar with.
