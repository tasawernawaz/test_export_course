To earn spending money, Jane “moonlights” in the food court at the student union during the evenings. She is the only person who works at the food court while she is there. She has noted that customers arrive at a rate of 10 per hour and that it takes her four minutes to serve a customer, on average. She assumes a Poisson distribution as a model of the arrival process and the exponential distribution as a a model of her service time distribution.
