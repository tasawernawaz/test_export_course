In this week, we will be introducing MATLAB to solve Practice Problems and Graded Problems. Specifically, we will be running an m-file via MATLAB Online, which solves for the production rate and average inventory of a deterministic two-machine line. Below is the list of inputs and outputs.

Inputs:

      1. \(r_1\): Repair rate of machine 1, also known as \(\frac{1}{MTTR_1}\)
      2. \(p_1\): Failure rate of machine 1, also known as \(\frac{1}{MTTF_1}\)
      3. \(r_2\): Repair rate of machine 2, also known as \(\frac{1}{MTTR_2}\)
      4. \(p_2\): Failure rate of machine 2, also known as \(\frac{1}{MTTF_2}\)
      5. \(N\): Buffer size

Outputs:

      1. prodrate: Production rate of the line
      2. \(\bar{n}\): Average buffer level
